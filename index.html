AI OR US?

This project explores the interaction between human and AI-generated content. It focuses on how AI can be used to create scripts for films, and how actors, directors, and viewers perceive the difference between AI-written and human-written material. Through interactive voting, users can guess who each person believes is AI.

AI OR US also highlights the potential impact of AI on artistic industries, particularly film, and explores whether AI could become a tool for enhancing creativity or a threat to employment in the arts.

The voting system tracks how many people voted for each character.
